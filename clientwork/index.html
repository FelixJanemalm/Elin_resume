Client work

Social Media Campaign
The City Of Joondalup

Developed a social media strategy with creative content from a brief. Pitched to the client the following:

Message: Inspiring new memories

Based on the current focus that Joondalup, described in their brief, has on educating and attract visitors to Joondalup. The content is developed specifically for each media channel, with the objective to create engagement and a following.

Promotional material such as the example on left is only to be posted in Stories to keep feed cleaner with just the big campaigns that gives more value to the viewer. Promotional material will service as reminders, which is perfect for Stories.

On Facebook. A Collaboration video series teaming up with people and businesses of Joondalup. Each video features an intriguing storyline from a person living in Joondalup or an insight into a business. A business could be a restaurant demonstrating how to cook one of their dishes. Example of a person is photographer Simon Yipp.

On Instagram. Each post will consist of a video portrait of a location or activity in Joondalup, building a visual portfolio. High-Quality visual content which fits with the target audience usage of Instagram.

The content is focused on being inspirational as well as focusing on the customer’s problem which is lack of ideas of day or weekend activities in the area.

“Meet” Facebook Campaign
Kph Trycksaksbolaget

“Meet” video series introducing the employees at Kph. Research had shown that trust was the single biggest concern when choosing a manufacturing partner. I developed the strategy to build that trust and relationship before the first contact by showcasing the heart, kindness and passion that the person has for their job and their clients. The video series had an average organic reach of 800 per video with an increase of between 3-10 new contacts after each video was released.
